Our Process

Four specialized AI research agents work in parallel to build a complete picture of your digital presence.

1

Research

AI agents simultaneously collect data across SEO, technical health, social media, reviews, local search, and competitive intelligence.

2

Analyze

Raw data is cross-referenced and validated. Competitor gaps are identified. Keyword opportunities are scored by difficulty and ROI potential.

3

Score

Your digital presence is scored across 5 categories using weighted metrics. Each finding is classified as critical, moderate, or positive.

4

Report

Findings are translated into plain-language insights with prioritized recommendations. Every gap is mapped to a specific, actionable solution.
